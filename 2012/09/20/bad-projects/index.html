How to survive on a bad projects.

Bad project usually means something. Why it happens?
Thousands of reasons It could be communications or bad management,
stupid developers or or …

if you imagine international project where people are distributed around the Globe and
came from different places, how will it look like?

Lets imagine we have a project in a bank and team members are located or from multiple countries:
Cyprus, India, Germany, Czech Republic, Ukraine, Poland, Israel, Romania, Finland, Sweden.

Everyone have different traditions different communication style and culture.
Teams are distributed and sometimes never seen each other.

Running such project is like to build the Tower of Babel.

It takes time to get good results from such team and make it “manageable”.
But before such a project could have couple of issues to overcome.

How to survive in such tough project if you are lucky to be there ???

Here are few recommendations I found from my own experience useful:

0 - Relax, prepare to the worth scenario
Difficult projects are antipodes to Agile.
Instead of 1-2 month and continuous delivery
they can take 2-3 years before first release

1 - Defects and bugs
Those are common way how to manage requirements on such projects
Even when your delivery system is not perfect
It’a a bad idea to change it in the middle

2 - Become master of your defects
Use TDD and Learn how to work with Legacy code. It will save a lot of time

3 - Learn new things every day.
Do reading-book breaks regularly.
Use inefficiency of your project and free time to improve knowledge.

4 - Educate your colleagues. Be the source.
Learn from others. Help each other to use best tools and be more efficient.

5 - Be positive
Long running projects may break relationships.
Relationships in business are more important then emotions

6 - Be professional
There are always possibilities to make small improvements in the process.
Expand your knowledge about your colleagues in multiculture environment.

7 - Don’t fight with the system.
Instead of changing existing system try to learn how it works.

8 - Make good relationship with your managers and colleagues.
Relationship for the future are more important.
It’s good way to be promoted later and be again on the next project.
Fighters are usually not invited

9 - Keep good relations with customer.
Don’t shit about your company.
Promote it by doing your job and helping your client to get things done.

10 - Keep good health conditions by doing sport and eating healthy food.
It’s one of the most required stress killing recommendation.
Try to get your team to play any active games you could play together.
