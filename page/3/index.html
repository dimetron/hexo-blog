How to survive on a bad projects.

Bad project usually means something. Why it happens?
Thousands of reasons It could be communications or bad management,
stupid developers or or …

if you imagine international project where people are distributed around the Globe and
came from different places, how will it look like?

Lets imagine we have a project in a bank and team members are located or from multiple countries:
Cyprus, India, Germany, Czech Republic, Ukraine, Poland, Israel, Romania, Finland, Sweden.

Everyone have different traditions different communication style and culture.
Teams are distributed and sometimes never seen each other.

Running such project is like to build the Tower of Babel.

It takes time to get good results from such team and make it “manageable”.
But before such a project could have couple of issues to overcome.
